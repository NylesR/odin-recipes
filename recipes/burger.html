A Recipe for a Burger

Description

America's staple food. A burger is a sandwich consisting of one or more cooked patties of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun. The patty may be pan-fried, grilled, or flame-broiled. Burgers are often garnished with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese and condiments such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, and relish.

Ingredients:

  • Burger Bun
  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
