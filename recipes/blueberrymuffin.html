A Recipe for Blueberry Muffins

Description

Blueberry muffins are a quinessential fruit based muffin to make. A blueberry muffin is a type of baked good made with flour, sugar, butter or oil, eggs, milk, and blueberries. Blueberry muffins can be either sweet or savory, and are often served as a breakfast or brunch food. Blueberry muffins can be enjoyed plain or with butter, jam, or cream cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Blueberries
